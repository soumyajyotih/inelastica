Na BCC¶

Phonon band structure for Na BCC (see Examples/Phonon_bands/Na_BCC).

Lattice parameter a = 4.23 Å and a 7x7x7 repetition of the primitive cell. Main computational settings:

PAO.EnergyShift 0.01 Ry
PAO.BasisSize   DZP
XC.functional   GGA
XC.authors      PBE
MeshCutoff      500. Ry
MD.FCDispl      0.02 Ang
